Overview

Release Date: October 1, 2021 (United States)

Director: Andy Serkis

Genre: Action, Sci-fi

Plot: In 1996, a young Cletus Kasady witnesses his lover, Frances Barrison, being taken to the Ravencroft Institute after an incident involving her sonic scream powers. Fast forward to the present day, Detective Mulligan asks journalist Eddie Brock to speak to Cletus in prison, leading to a series of events where Venom, Eddie's alien symbiote, plays a crucial role. As Cletus's execution begins, a red symbiote named Carnage emerges, causing chaos and forming an alliance with Cletus to eliminate Eddie and Venom. The story unfolds with intense confrontations, including Venom devouring Carnage and killing Cletus while the collapsing cathedral crushes Frances. Mulligan's survival is revealed as his eyes glow blue, hinting at possible powers. In a mid-credits scene, Venom and Eddie find themselves in another universe watching news about Spider-Man's revealed identity as Peter Parker.
